As a company, your priority is good customer service and the guarantee is that promise that reaffirms your commitment to quality.


This is how DTC emerges, we provide intelligent technology to improve your after-sales services through the digitalization of your company's warranty processes.


We can help you connect with your customers through a simple, friendly software adapted to the particularities of your business. DTC allows you to quickly and easily access the company's warranty data. With this you can offer greater comfort and security to your customers when purchasing your products services.

STEP I


We facilitate the digitization of your warranty process with our intelligent DTC system. We advise you in the process of receiving and uploading data.


STEP II


Receive your custom DTC software. Enter with your mail to our secure system. Only you have your access.


STEP III


Your customers enter DTC easily and intuitively. A smart bot integrated into the system guides you through the process.


STEP IV


Manage and validate guarantees at any time, from your PC or mobile with our personalized app. Receive notifications of the status of the data.
